\subsection*{Figures and tables}
Figures require a descriptive caption that should be further explained in the text; e.g., what is being plotted, what is important about the plot, and what is the main takeaway from the plot. In general, the figures are not explained adequately. Similarly with tables. 

\subsection*{Equations}
The parameters in an equation should be defined with units, even if it is obvious. For example, clearly, $\rho$ is density, but the units thereof could be $kg/m^3$, $g/cm^3$, etc.
